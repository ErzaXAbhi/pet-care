About Us

There are so many challenges that come with finding a good, local pet sitter. You need to find someone both you and your pet trust. You need them to be available on the right days at the right times, and maybe even last minute. And of course, you want them to be experienced and great with pets. At Fetch, these are the problems we solve for. Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for you to schedule and receive pet care with the ideal sitter for you.
